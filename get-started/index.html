Getting Started

Brightcove Audience is used to track video engagement data for videos that are viewed inside of an Audience-enabled Brightcove Player. Viewing data is stored in Video Cloud and can optionally be synchronized to popular marketing automation platforms such as Eloqua, Marketo, HubSpot and Salesforce.
