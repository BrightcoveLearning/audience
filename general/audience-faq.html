Brightcove Audience FAQ

This topic contains some of the common questions asked about Brightcove Audience.

Why Audience & How it Works

Why do I need to track video in my marketing automation solution?
Marketing automation solutions do not track video today. If you are investing in video as a powerful piece of content, you should make sure you are tracking its effectiveness and automating communication with leads/users who view your content.
Does Brightcove and the Audience module make use of any cookies?
The Audience module creates and uses a 3rd party cookie to identify users so that viewers don’t get asked to fill out a lead form a second time. The module also makes use of Marketo and Eloqua APIs which themselves use cookies to track viewers.
Does Brightcove and the Audience module make use of any cookies?
The Audience module creates and uses a 3rd party cookie to identify users when an integration is in place. This way viewers don’t get asked to fill out a lead form a second time. The module also makes use of Marketo, HubSpot and Eloqua APIs which themselves use cookies to track viewers. When using Audience Profiles we use a Brightcove cookie.
Does every Brightcove customer get this product?
No. Audience is available as a Purchasable Add-on to Video Marketing Suite customers. Audience Profiles, however, is available to every Brightcove customer for free. You can think of it as “Audience-lite”
Who is eligible for Audience Profiles.
Starting Q4 2017 every VMS customer will have access to Audience Profiles at no cost. They will need to activate it to start tracking data. EVS customers will have Audience SSO tracking available to them as well.

Viewer Tracking & Limitations

What limitations does Audience Profiles have compared to the full-blown Audience?
No integrating with any third party systems, no API access, no custom forms (Audience form only), 1000 views/24 hour period capture cap. You can still manually export your data to a CSV or XLS file.
What limitations does Audience have when connected to a marketing automation platform?
???
Are there any limits on how often the Audience API can be polled? I know there's a 1000 cap on video search results, but are there other frequency related caps to that API endpoint?
The max # of view events per API call is actually 100. There’s no rate limiting in place that I know of but if you tried to call it too much it’d probably slow down quote a bit. The golf channel had trouble getting out lots of data via the API. In fact they couldn’t get it out faster than they were putting it in.
Is there any way to get Audience data faster than the 3 hour delay that is currently present? They want real-time metrics and can't get that with the Audience analytics.
No. If they want real-time metrics they’d need to write their own plugin and send data to their own system.
What is the trigger for an event view to be sent to Audience's data? Is it when the video completes, or if the player window is closed, or something else? What happens if the user never completes the video and doesn't close their player window?
It varies but basically the Audience plugin sends a heartbeat back to the server every 10 seconds.
When does audience consider tracking on a view to be "completed"- Is it when the player window is closed, or if they complete the video? What happens if they watch part way through, pause the video and never close the player window?
it’s when 15 minutes have passed since we last say a heart beat, basically lack of activity.
Is it possible for viewer data to change once it's available in the Audience viewing data? Will that event data ever be updated in the future my some kind of log consolidation?
Once the data is available in Audience via UI or API it’s “settled” it’s not going to change.

Data protection & record retention

Is Audience GDPR compliant?

Yes, Audience gives customers the ability to act on behalf of their users, looking up and purging any data they’ve collected via Audience.

https://support.brightcove.com/managing-viewer-data

How long can data be retained in Audience?
MAP integrations can be set to a maximum of 6 months and non-MAP integrations can be set to 3 years.
How long are GUID lookups cached?
30 days for known and 7 days for unknown (only applies to MAP integrations, no lookups needed for non-MAP integrations)

Marketing automation & viewer analytics

What is different about tracking video from any other media in my marketing automation platform?
As opposed to a webpage or downloadable white paper, with a video, you can know exactly how much of the video they watched and therefore exactly what content they have been exposed to.
How do you connect with my Eloqua/Marketo/Hubspot account?
You are able to log into your Eloqua/Marketo/HubSpot account directly through the Video Marketing Suite in the Audience module. From there you are able to create players and lead forms with MAP tracking built in so you can start tracking video analytics immediately.
Can I track views on my Eloqua/Marketo/HubSpot pages as well as my non-Eloqua/Marketo/HubSpot landing pages (sitecore/Wordpress/Gallery etc)?
Yes, as long as the player being used is “Audience enabled” (has the Audience plugin installed) all view will be tracked whether it resides on a MAP landing page or any other landing page.
What can I track with this integration?
With our integration, you can track video viewing analytics (video, video ID, percent viewed, time watched) for known contacts already in your database.
What about unknown contacts? Can I track them too?
With our integration, you can track unknown contacts and then when they submit an Eloqua/HubSpot form, we are able to associate their viewership information with that new contact retroactively. Audience resubmits viewership information daily to match view data from unknown contacts back to your Oracle Eloqua/HubSpot instance.
Where will my video-view data show up in my MAP?
This depends on which MAP you are using. See the following documentation for Oracle Eloqua, Marketo and HubSpot
Where in Salesforce will my video viewing data show up?
Video views are delivered into Salesforce as a task under the viewer’s contact in the Activity section. See “Viewing video data in Salesforce for an example of this.
Can I be notified with SalesForce when one of my contacts has watched videos?
Not directly. However, you can run reports that would notify you of a contact’s activity.
Is there any difference between tracking with Salesforce vs. Oracle Eloqua/Marketo?
The biggest difference is that Salesforce does not use Cookies to track users. Therefore the customer must manually provide a unique identifier. See this documentation for more info. Additionally, SalesForce, being a CRM does not offer automated follow-ups or lead scoring. However, you can create tasks in SalesForce which are then assigned to your sales team for real-life follow-ups.
Audience has a “Custom” integration option, what other platforms can Audience integrate with using this?

Audience can “integrate” with any data platform that:

  1. Uses a unique identifier for each contact record
  2. Has an API that can receive additional data from third-party platforms (like Audience)

Player Lead Forms

When does Audience display a lead-capture form?
The default setting only displays the lead-capture form to an unknown user in your MAP database (the ones you want to convert into a known user). You can override this in the settings if you choose to always display the lead-form. This is most useful for testing. Known users are automatically tracked and that data is associated with the contact in your MAP without the need to display a form.
Can I customize Brightcove forms?
You can adjust the text and fields displayed per the UI. You can not modify it beyond what is available in the UI (Gallery can update some styling to make the forms match the site)

General topics

Which embed code should I use? Iframe or in-page?
For Audience to work it is required that the “Advanced” embed code is used. If the “Standard” (or iFrame) embed code is used, then the player is isolated from the page which means the Audience plugin cannot see the unique identifier and thus all users will be seen as “unknown”.
What is marketing automation?
Marketing automation is a technology that tracks lead/user interaction with different online marketing channels (email, web, forms, content, video, social...etc.), help them market more effectively across these channels, and automate repetitive marketing tasks (such as email communications). Marketing automation allows the marketer to setup automated and personalized tasks based on predefined variables. For Example: If a user watches 75% of a specific video, send them an email on that specific subject but if they only watch 12% send them something else.
Is Brightcove a marketing automation company?
No, Brightcove provides a complete solution for video marketing. With our solution, you can get your videos online, organize your content, create players, create video portals, track analytics...and integrate with your own existing marketing automation platform. We integrate video analytics into marketing automation platforms.
How can I check to see if a customer’s Marketo custom activity is setup correctly?
Audience has a “hidden” URL which offers some debug tools for certain kinds of integrations, Marketo REST being one. https://studio.brightcove.com/products/videocloud/audience/debug
What does the customer have to do for a custom integration?
They will need to do three things. Expose a unique identifier in the URL or code of the page that Audience can capture Write (or use BGS’s services) a script that makes use of our Audience API’s to retrieve the data as a JSON file Map that data to fields that they have created in their destination-database (think spreadsheet merge) If a customer is interested in setting up a custom integration send them the following support documentation to review and assess if they can do it on their own or if they need additional help. Creating Custom Integrations with the Audience Module: https://support.brightcove.com/creating-custom-integrations-audience-module - This is how you will provide a unique identifier that Audience can capture. Audience API: https://support.brightcove.com/audience-api - This is how you’d programmatically retrieve all the data as a JSON file which you can then import into your data processing system of choice.
How does Audience push CDO data to Eloqua?
We use the custom object bulk API. https://docs.oracle.com/cloud/latest/marketingcs_gs/OMCAC/api-bulk-2.0-customobjects.html
How does Audience push the external asset activity? How can I control the Campaign, External Asset and Activity Types used?
We using the external activity API. https://docs.oracle.com/cloud/latest/marketingcs_gs/OMCAC/op-api-rest-2.0-data-activity-post.html There's no control over the name of the campaign, asset name or type. We simply create a campaign on our own (named BrightcoveVideoPlay) and then proceed to create activities also named BrightcoveVideoPlay.
